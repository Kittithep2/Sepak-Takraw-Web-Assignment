Sepak Takraw

Sepak Takraw may have been introduced to Southeast Asia by the Chinese,
who were inspired by the traditional game Cuju,
an ancient military exercise where soldiers play to keep a leather ball filled with feathers airborne by kicking it back and forth between two or more people.
As the game evolved, the feather-stuffed ball was replaced by an air-filled ball with a two-layered hull.
Cuju is also considered by the International Football Association (FIFA) as the origin of football as a sport.
